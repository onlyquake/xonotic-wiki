h1. Clan Arena (CA)

h2. Object of the Game

Clan Arena is basically Team [[Last_Man_Standing|LMS]] (you spawn with almost all weapons again). Again 2 teams (red & blue) fight against each other. Once you die you have to spectate. If 1 team is 100% dead the other team gets 1 point. 10 points to win a map.
