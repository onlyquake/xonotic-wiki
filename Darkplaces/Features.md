DarkPlaces Wiki
===============

Features
========

Graphics Features
-----------------

-   Realtime Dynamic Lighting
-   Diffuse, Normal, Gloss & Glow Mapping
-   Omnidirectional Shadow Mapping
-   [Scriptable particle effects](./Effectinfo)
-   [Skeletal Animation](./Modeling)
-   [Scriptable Surface Materials](./Materials)
-   Reflective/Refractive Water

Supported Formats
-----------------

### Models

-   MDL (Quake 1)
-   MD2 (Quake 2)
-   MD3 (Quake 3)
-   PSK (Unreal)
-   ZYM
-   DPM
-   IQM
-   OBJ

### Maps

-   Quake 1 (BSP29)
-   Quake 2 (BSP38)
-   Quake 3 (BSP46)
-   Quake Live (BSP47)
-   IronGrip:Warlord (BSP48)
-   Half-Life (BSP30)

### Images

-   WAL (Quake 2)
-   TGA
-   PNG
-   JPG

### Sounds

-   WAV
-   OGG
-   CD Audio
